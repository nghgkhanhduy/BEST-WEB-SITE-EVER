All About Keyboards

Keyboards come in various styles, each catering to different user preferences and needs. Each type has its unique advantages, making the choice largely dependent on personal preference and specific use cases.

Explore the different types of keyboards: Chicklet, Membrane, and Mechanical.

Chicklet Keyboard

Chicklet keyboards are known for their flat, square-shaped keys with a low profile. They're commonly used in laptops and are known for being slim and quiet.

Membrane Keyboard

Membrane keyboards use a thin, flexible membrane layer under the keys. They're affordable and provide a soft typing experience but may lack tactile feedback.

Mechanical Keyboard

Mechanical keyboards are popular for their durable switches and tactile feedback. They offer various switch types catering to different preferences.
