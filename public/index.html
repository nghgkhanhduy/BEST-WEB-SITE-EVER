ALL ABOUT PHYSICAL KEYBOARD

​

Keyboards come in various styles, each catering to different user preferences

and needs. Each type has its unique advantages, making the choice largely

dependent on personal preference and specific use cases.

Explore the different types of keyboards: Chicklet, Membrane, and Mechanical.

.............​

𝙲𝚑𝚒𝚌𝚕𝚎𝚝 Keyboard

Chiclet keyboards are known for their flat, square-shaped keys with a low profile. They're commonly used in laptops and are known for being slim and quiet.

𝕸𝖊𝖒𝖇𝖗𝖆𝖓𝖊 Keyboard

Membrane keyboards use a thin, flexible membrane layer under the keys. They're affordable and provide a soft typing experience but may lack tactile feedback. Also, Chiclet have membrane mechanics.

𝓜𝓮𝓬𝓱𝓪𝓷𝓲𝓬𝓪𝓵 Keyboard

Mechanical keyboards are popular for their durable switches and tactile feedback. They offer various switch types catering to different preferences. However, the only downside is the prices when most of them range at >$50.
